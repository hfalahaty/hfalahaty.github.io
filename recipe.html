Ghorme

  • Time: 2 houres
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
goosht 0.5 kilo
loobia 250 gr
sabzi 250 gr
ab 2 li

Step 1: sabzi ra sorkh konid
Step 2: goosht, loobia, sabzi va ab ra dar ghablame berizid
Step 3: bad az 2 saat ghaza hazer ast

Ash

  • Time: 3 houres
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
nokhod 250 gr
loobia 250 gr
reshte 100 gr
ab 2 li

Step 1: nokhod va loobia ra dar ab rikhte yek shab khis bokhorad
Step 2: ab rakhali konid va nokhod va loobia ra dar ab berizid ta bepazad
Step 3: bad az do saat va nim reshte ra ezafe konid
Step 4: bad az se saat ghaza hazer ast
